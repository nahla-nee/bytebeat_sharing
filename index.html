Info

Bytebeats and floatbeats are a form of low-complexity art where in music is generated through algorithmic manipulation of a continously incrementing variable, though there are many definitions and restrictions that vary from person to person. This type of art draws inspiration from the demoscene and is focused on making art out of very minimal resources. If you would like to know more about them, then check out the info page

Settings

Share

To allow you control over your shared music without making an account, we allow you to simply set a password per beat. If you want to update your beat simply insert the beat's name and password and hit share. If it already exists, and your password is correct, you'll update it. If it doesn't exist, you'll create it! Think of this as your username and password in a way. But not really. Don't use an important password to you here, this stuff isn't hashed, it's just straight clear text.
